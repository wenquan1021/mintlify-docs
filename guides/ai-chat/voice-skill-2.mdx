---
title: "Voice Skill 2: Message"
---

Mumble helps you send messages to your WhatsApp contacts using your voice, so you can reach out without typing or switching apps.

Follow the steps below to set it up and get started:

1. Go to Settings
2. Turn on WhatsApp access. You’ll be redirected to a web page with a QR code

![Help Doc Voice Skill 2](/images/help_doc_voice-skill_2.png)

3. Follow the instructions on the web page to scan the QR code and connect your WhatsApp account with Mumble
4. Return to the app, press “option” + “space”, or select “Message” from the Voice Skills, and tell Mumble:
   - who you want to message on WhatsApp, and
   - what message you want to send.

   Mumble will take care of the rest.
