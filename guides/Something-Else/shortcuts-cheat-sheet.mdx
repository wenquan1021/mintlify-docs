---
title: "Shortcuts Cheat Sheet"
---

At Mumble, you can use shortcuts anywhere in the app to trigger chat and voice actions quickly. Below are the default shortcuts available in the app.

**Hold Option**

Holding Option lets you access the following actions:

- AI Chat
- Voice Dictation
- Voice Edit

**Press “option” + “space”**

After pressing the shortcut, a panel will appear with the following options:

- Up arrow – Add your voice to a voice note
- Right arrow – Trigger a voice skill, and Mumble will take action based on your request
- Down arrow – Switch to chat and talk with Mumble
