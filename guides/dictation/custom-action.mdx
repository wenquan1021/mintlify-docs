---
title: "Set up a custom action for the dictation panel"
---

1. Go to **Settings \> Dictation**
2. Toggle on “**Custom ↓ action prompt”**

   <Frame>
     ![Screenshot 2026 03 08 At 6 48 47 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-08at6.48.47PM.png)
   </Frame>
3. Enter your prompt in the text area (e.g. "Rewrite the following as a formal email")
4. During dictation, press **“↓”** to apply your custom transformation

If Custom is not configured, the **“↓”** option appears grayed out in the panel.
