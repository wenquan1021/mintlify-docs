**Use the Chat in the Way That Works Best for You**

You can start a voice comment at any moment with the shortcut key. This reduces context switching and keeps your workflow smooth and uninterrupted.

More voice skills will continue to roll out, helping you think faster and stay productive with less effort!

**Message Input Toolbar**

The message bar gives you flexible ways to share content with Mumble. It supports quick uploads and helps Mumble understand more context about your work.

You can use the toolbar to:

- drag and drop images into the chat
- attach local files from your device
