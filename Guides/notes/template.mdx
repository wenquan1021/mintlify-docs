---
title: "Template"
---

Templates help you create consistent, well structured content quickly. The same template library is used for meeting summaries, notes, and AI Drafts.

- Where to access and create templates

  You can create templates in two ways:
  - Under the note title, click the second button which shows “Auto” by default. Open it and select “Add new”. Tell Mumble what you want the template to look like and it will generate it for you
  - In the left navigation bar, open “Template” and click “+ New template” to create one
- How to add and apply a template to a note
  - Click Auto to open the template panel
  - Select the template you want, and Mumble will apply it immediately
