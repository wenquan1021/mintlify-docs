---
title: "Global dictation panel"
---

The global dictation panel lets you dictate text anywhere on your Mac and paste it into any app. Hold the Fn key to start, speak, then release to paste.

**How to use**

1. Make sure **“Dictation outside Mumble”** is enabled in **Settings \> Dictation** first.
2. Hold “**Fn“** and start speaking
3. While holding ”**Fn“**, press an arrow key to choose an action (optional)
4. Release ”**Fn“** to finish. The text is automatically pasted into the active app

**Keyboard shortcuts**

While holding Fn, press an arrow key to choose how your speech is processed. The selected action highlights in the panel.

- **“←” Left arrow key** → Clean up: Polishes your speech into clean text. Fixes grammar, punctuation, and formatting. This is the default process.

  <Frame>
    ![Dictation Default](/images/dictation_default-1.GIF)
  </Frame>
- **“↑” Up arrow key** → Bullets: Formats your speech as a bullet point list.

  <Frame>
    ![Dictation Bullet](/images/dictation_bullet.GIF)
  </Frame>
- **“→” Right arrow key** → Open: Extracts a URL from your speech and opens it in the browser.

  <Frame>
    ![Dictation Open](/images/dictation_open.GIF)
  </Frame>
- **“↓” Down arrow key** → Custom: Applies your custom prompt to transform the text (must be set up in Settings).

  <Frame>
    ![Dictation Custom](/images/dictation_custom.GIF)
  </Frame>
- **“, ” Comma** → Emoji: Cancels dictation and opens the macOS emoji keyboard.
- **“Esc”** → Cancel: Cancels dictation without pasting anything.
