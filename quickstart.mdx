    ![Screenshot 2025 12 26 At 1 06 39 PM](/images/Screenshot2025-12-26at1.06.39PM.png)

    - Microphone Access: lets Mumble to capture your voice during meetings and voice notes
    - System Audio Recording: allows Mumble to capture the voices of other meeting participants
    - Accessibility: helps Mumble to monitor your apps and automatically detect when a meeting begins
    - Screen Recording: enables Mumble to see your meeting screen and detect participants

    Microphone Access and System Audio Recording are the essential permissions for voice notes and meeting features. These permissions are used only for transcription and never accessed or shared beyond your recording sessions.
